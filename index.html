How to Play Spy (Imposter)

Secret word party game rules — quick and simple.

Goal: Everyone gets the same secret word… except one player (the Spy/Imposter). The group must find them. The Spy must blend in and (ideally) guess the word.

Setup

Create a room and invite friends. Start a round.

Round flow

1) Everyone sees a secret word — except the Spy, who sees “You are the Spy”.

2) Players take turns giving a short clue about the word (don’t say it directly).

3) After a few turns, talk it out: Who sounds fake? Who is too vague? Who is too specific?

4) Vote. The player with the most votes is revealed.

Who wins?

Group wins if you vote out the Spy.

Spy wins if they survive the votes — or if your version allows it: if they correctly guess the secret word.

Quick tips

Keep clues specific enough to prove you know the word, but not so obvious that the Spy can guess it.

If you’re the Spy: copy the vibe, ask questions, and stay calm. People overthink.
